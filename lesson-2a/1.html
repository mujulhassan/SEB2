What is CMS

CMS "content management system" (engine) - a basis for a site written by PHP programmers, with which you can manage the site (add content, change meny items, etc) without knowing HTML and CSS. However, in order to make a website using CMS you will need the services of a programmer, a designer, and a coder, And capital investment.
